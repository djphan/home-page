About Me

My name is Daniel and I'm a programmer with a background in the field of bioinformatics. I am interested in a wide range of fields such as software development , web design , and data analytics .

In terms of areas of research, I'm interested in the topics of genomics, systems biology, and combining genetics and computer sciences in an interdisciplinary fashion.

This web resume is a portfolio and sandbox for me to play around with some of the current technologies I see online. I'm avid learner and always looking to add to my skill set.
